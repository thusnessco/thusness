Resistance can show up as tension, a story about how things should be different, or a quiet refusal to feel what is present. It is not wrong—it is information. This week we sit with it plainly: not to fix it, but to see it clearly as it is.

When the edges of resistance are seen without blame, there is often more room than we expected.
